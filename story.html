The Story of Yoru & Mochi

Yoru & Mochi are two small companions who are always together. They love playing, resting, eating, and exploring their little world with endless curiosity.

Yoru has a darker color and a more serious expression, while Mochi looks brighter, gentle, and cheerful. This contrast is what makes them such a unique duo.

Every day brings something new — from tasting fresh vegetables, running around their play area, to quietly leaning on each other during nap time.

We share these small moments with the world to bring comfort, smiles, and a sense of calm to everyone who watches.
