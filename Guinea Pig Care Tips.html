Guinea Pig Care Tips for Beginners

A simple guide covering basic needs, diet, cleanliness, and health signs.

Guinea pigs are relatively easy to care for when basic principles are followed:

  • Proper housing: Provide a spacious cage with soft, clean bedding.
  • Healthy diet: Fresh hay daily, quality pellets, and vegetables.
  • Clean routine: Clean the enclosure several times a week.
  • Interaction time: Allow safe playtime and gentle interaction.
  • Health awareness: Watch for appetite loss or unusual behavior.
