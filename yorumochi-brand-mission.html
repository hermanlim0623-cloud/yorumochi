YoruMochi Brand Mission and Vision

Mission

To create gentle and uplifting content that highlights the charm of guinea pigs and encourages responsible pet care. YoruMochi aims to share small moments of joy through simple short videos and calm photography.

Vision

To become a trusted and warm destination for viewers who enjoy peaceful, positive, and pet-friendly content. The brand hopes to create a community where viewers feel connected and inspired.
