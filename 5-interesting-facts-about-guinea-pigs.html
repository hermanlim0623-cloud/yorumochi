5 Interesting Facts About Guinea Pigs

Here are five interesting facts about guinea pigs you should know, especially if you are considering them as pets.

  1. Highly social: Guinea pigs enjoy companionship and feel more comfortable with friends or human interaction.
  2. Unique sounds: They communicate through various sounds such as wheeking, purring, and chirping.
  3. Minimal digging behavior: Unlike some rodents, guinea pigs do not dig excessively.
