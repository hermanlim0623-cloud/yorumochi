What Is YoruMochi

YoruMochi is a small creative brand built around the guinea pig duo Yoru and Mochi. The brand focuses on short daily videos, calm photo moments, and simple care tips for pet owners. YoruMochi began as a way to document happy moments and soon became a friendly space for people who enjoy peaceful and positive content.

You can visit the official website here: https://yorumochi.com

The name YoruMochi came from combining the two characters. Yoru is energetic and curious, while Mochi is gentle and calm. Together, they create a comforting and cheerful experience for viewers.
