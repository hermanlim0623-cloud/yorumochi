3 Common Myths About Guinea Pigs

Many beliefs about guinea pigs are misleading ‚Äî here are three common myths and the real facts.

  1. Myth #1: Guinea pigs are aggressive
    Fact: Guinea pigs are gentle animals and rarely bite when handled calmly.
  2. Myth #2: They are difficult to care for
    Fact: With proper food, clean housing, and attention, guinea pigs are relatively easy to care for.
  3. Myth #3: They need extremely large cages
    Fact: They need enough space to move comfortably, but a clean, well-sized enclosure is sufficient.
