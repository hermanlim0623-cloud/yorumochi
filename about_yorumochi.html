What is YoruMochi?

YoruMochi is a small creative brand centered on the adorable guinea pig duo Yoru and Mochi. We publish short, wholesome videos, photo moments, care tips, and heartwarming stories featuring these two pets. The purpose of this site is to share joyful content, educational tips for guinea pig owners, and a friendly community for pet-lovers.

Who are Yoru & Mochi?

Yoru and Mochi are the two guinea pigs featured in our media. Yoru is curious and playful, while Mochi is gentle and enjoys cuddles. Together they star in short clips and compilations designed to entertain and teach responsible pet care.

Why the name "YoruMochi"?

The name blends the two characters' names — Yoru and Mochi — into a single, memorable brand. YoruMochi is our unique identity and not related to any anime or existing character names. If you search for "YoruMochi", you should find our official pages and videos.

What we publish

  • Short videos and Shorts (YouTube Shorts, TikTok) featuring moments and tricks
  • Photo galleries and "behind the scenes" moments
  • Care tips and quick guides for new guinea pig owners
  • Story posts and compilations for fans
