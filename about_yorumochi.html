What is YoruMochi?

YoruMochi is a small creative brand centered on the guinea pig duo Yoru and Mochi. We make short, wholesome clips, photography moments, and short guides about guinea pig care. Our goal is to share joy, teach gentle pet care, and build a friendly community for pet lovers.

Who are Yoru & Mochi?

Yoru is the curious explorer — playful and energetic. Mochi is calm and cuddly — often the gentle star of relaxed moments. Together they appear in daily shorts, compilations, and themed content that highlight pet behaviour and responsible care.

Why the name "YoruMochi"?

The name combines the two characters' names — Yoru and Mochi — into one memorable brand. YoruMochi is our original identity and not associated with other characters or franchises.
