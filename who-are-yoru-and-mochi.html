Who Are Yoru and Mochi

You can visit the official website here: https://yorumochi.com

Yoru is the energetic explorer. He loves discovering new spots, trying new foods, and reacting to sounds. His playful nature makes him the active half of the duo.

Mochi is calm and gentle. She enjoys relaxing moments, soft bedding, and quiet interaction. Mochi is often the center of peaceful scenes in videos.

Fun facts

  • Yoru reacts strongly to new sounds.
  • Mochi enjoys slow blinking and calm spaces.
  • Both love fresh vegetables and hay time.
  • They often synchronize movements when relaxed.
